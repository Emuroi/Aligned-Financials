Money in and money out

How it works

Positive amounts are treated as money in. Negative amounts are treated as money out. Separate debit and credit columns are also detected automatically, then you can refine category, review state, and reconciliation below.

Best statement format

CSV files with columns like Date, Description, Amount, Debit, Credit, Paid In, or Paid Out will import most reliably.
